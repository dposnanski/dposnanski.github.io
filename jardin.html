el jardín vegano

Online e-commerce site dedicated to promoting vegan products across Mexico. The project was built on WordPress and, besides working on the theme customization, I also translated the products’ description to Spanish.

El Jardín Vegano was born on April 2014 to provide delicious alternatives to vegans in Mexico. For this project, I customized a theme to make it colorful and to match the playfulness of the logo.

The user experience was one of the most important aspects of the site, so product information such as expiration date, size, weight, ingredients, and nutritional information were added to each product.

A review functionality and wishlist were also implemented. I worked on this project for two years before moving abroad.
