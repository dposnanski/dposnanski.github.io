nocturnalia

Online e-commerce site dedicated to selling stickers, cups and other items with nocturnal-looking designs. The project was built on WordPress and, for this project, I customized a theme so it would be captivating and mysterious, while feminine and inviting.

This online store features a clean design, a vertical navigation bar, FAQs, and Google Maps to locate the store.
